About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Educational Technology at Beijing Normal University [Our Lab]. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIEd) and Computerized Adaptive Testing (CAT). Since "every child born has innate goodness", no matter how technology develops, I believe that technology will always be beneficial to education.

Research Interests

  • Automatic Item Generation
  • Knowledge Tracing
  • LLM Benchmarking for Educational Purposes

Projects

  • MixIO - an education-first open-source IoT platform [Gitee Repo] [Official Server]

    • I’m proud to share that MixIO began in 2020 as my undergraduate “Computer Networks” course project and has, under Prof. Qian Fu's support and our continuous contribution, grown into the open-source, education-first IoT platform you see today. The official server has around 3,000 active users per month. In addition, many primary and secondary school teachers in China have deployed our open-source server in their schools as a solution for the IoT section in information technology courses.
